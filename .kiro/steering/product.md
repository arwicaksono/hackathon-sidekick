# Product Overview

## Product Purpose
Hackathon Sidekick is an AI-powered assistant that democratizes hackathon success by helping anyone - regardless of coding experience - analyze ideas, track progress against judging rubrics, and automate documentation. It bridges the gap between great ideas and winning submissions by providing structured guidance throughout the hackathon process.

## Target Users
- **Hackathon participants** (especially non-coders who have great ideas but lack technical execution experience)
- **Founders** looking to validate and develop their startup ideas in hackathon environments
- **Designers and Product Managers** who want to lead technical teams effectively
- **Anyone** who wants to maximize their chances of hackathon success through systematic approach

## Key Features
- **Idea Evaluator** - Analyzes hackathon ideas against judging criteria and provides improvement suggestions
- **Project Initializer** - Sets up project structure, documentation templates, and development workflows
- **Progress Tracker** - Monitors development progress against hackathon timelines and judging rubrics
- **Documentation Assistant** - Automates creation of README, pitch decks, and submission materials
- **Rubric Alignment** - Ensures projects meet all judging criteria throughout development
- **Time Management** - Helps prioritize features and manage hackathon time constraints

## Business Objectives
- Increase hackathon win rates for non-technical participants
- Democratize access to structured development processes
- Reduce time spent on documentation and project setup
- Improve overall hackathon submission quality across the ecosystem

## User Journey
1. **Idea Input** - User describes their hackathon idea and goals
2. **Analysis & Feedback** - Sidekick evaluates idea against typical judging criteria
3. **Project Setup** - Automated initialization of project structure and documentation
4. **Guided Development** - Progress tracking with rubric-aligned milestones
5. **Documentation Generation** - Automated creation of submission materials
6. **Final Review** - Pre-submission evaluation and optimization recommendations

## Success Criteria
- Users report higher hackathon placement rates
- Reduced time from idea to structured project (target: <30 minutes)
- Improved documentation quality scores in hackathon feedback
- High user satisfaction with guidance and automation features
- Adoption by hackathon organizers as recommended tool
